# Do We All Live in Filter Bubbles on Social Media?
### Research Paper – Ethical Considerations in Data Science

This repository contains my research paper examining how algorithmic personalization shapes the information we see on social media. The study explores how filter bubbles form, how they influence user behavior, and the ethical challenges raised by AI-driven content curation.

##  Overview
This research discusses:
- How algorithmic filtering and personalization work  
- The psychological and societal impact of filter bubbles  
- Ethical issues such as transparency, autonomy, and bias  
- The responsibility of digital platforms in providing informational diversity  
- How users can navigate digital spaces more consciously  

The work connects to broader questions in **AI ethics, human behavior, and digital communication**.
